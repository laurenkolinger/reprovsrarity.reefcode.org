The prior benthic-code cross-reference omitted UAGA, so VINPS Agaricia agaricites cover was dropped. We re-dated the cross-reference table to benthicCodes_temp_29Jun2026.csv and mapped Agaricia agaricites to VINPS code UAGA, restoring 1070 VINPS transect-records of this species.
